Create a 3x3 grid for a high-end commercial marketing campaign, centered around Alexander McQueen men's luxury sneakers.

Each frame should present a unique visual concept while maintaining perfect product consistency across all nine images.

Grid Concepts (one per cell):

1. 'Iconic Hero Shot': Eye-level side view on a dark, glossy pedestal, emphasizing the signature oversized sole.

2. 'Extreme Macro Detail': Close-up of the heel or stitching, highlighting the top-tier leather texture and the 'Alexander McQueen' logo embossing.

3. 'Dynamic Interaction': The shoe stepping onto a surface, creating a subtle and elegant splash effect of black liquid or marble powder.

4. 'Minimalist Sculptural Feel': The shoe artistically balanced on a geometric concrete or marble block, emphasizing the architectural design.

5. 'Floating Lightness': The shoe suspended in the air with dynamic hanging laces, conveying supreme comfort and lightness.

6. 'Sensory Texture': A tactile atmosphere shot focusing on the contrast between the velvet or smooth leather upper and a rough stone texture.

7. 'Monochromatic Luxury': A color-block scene where the background perfectly matches the shoe's accent color, creating a bold editorial look.

8. 'Deconstructed Craftsmanship': An abstract arrangement of raw materials (leather swatches, rubber textures) surrounding the shoe, symbolizing high-quality construction.

9. 'Surreal Fusion': The shoe integrated into a surreal, dreamlike landscape, blending realism with high fashion.

Visual Rules:
* 'Subject': Alexander McQueen men's oversized sneakers.
* 'Consistency': The shoe's shape, proportion, sole thickness, logo placement, and typography must be 100% accurate.
* 'No Distortion': No deformation of the signature sole or branding.
* 'Separation': Clear separation between the shoe and the background.

Lighting and Style:
* Soft, controlled studio lighting with dramatic rim light to highlight the shoe's silhouette.
* Subtle highlights on the leather, deep realistic shadows under the sole.
* High dynamic range, 8K resolution, ultra-sharp focus.
* Editorial luxury fashion advertising aesthetic (Vogue/GQ style).
* Color Palette: Minimalist, monochromatic, metallic, and deep matte tones.

Overall Vibe:
* Modern, sophisticated, visually coherent.
* High-end commercial campaign for a luxury fashion brand.
* Surreal, cinematic, refined, and aspirational.
