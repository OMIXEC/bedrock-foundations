### Pro-Tips for Nano Banana 2 Realism

* **Specify the Lens:** Wide angles (14mm-24mm) distort and create drama. Portrait lenses (50mm-85mm) create flattering, realistic proportions.
* **Mention Lighting by Name:** Use terms like *Rembrandt lighting, butterfly lighting, volumetric haze, bounce light*, or *diffused softbox*. Lighting is 80% of what makes a photo look real.
* **Embrace Imperfections:** Real photos aren't perfectly smooth. Add words like *film grain, slight chromatic aberration, dust motes, weathered texture*, or *asymmetrical features* to trick the eye into seeing absolute reality.
