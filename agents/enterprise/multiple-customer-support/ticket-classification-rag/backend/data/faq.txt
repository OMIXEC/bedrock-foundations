# CloudSync Pro - Frequently Asked Questions

## Billing & Subscription

### How do I upgrade my plan?
You can upgrade your plan anytime through the web dashboard:
1. Log in to https://app.cloudsync.com
2. Go to Settings > Billing
3. Click "Change Plan"
4. Select your new plan and confirm
5. Changes take effect immediately
6. You'll be prorated for the current billing period

### How do I downgrade my plan?
To downgrade your plan:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select a lower-tier plan
4. Confirm downgrade
5. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing period
6. Ensure your usage fits within new plan limits before downgrade

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo
- PayPal (for annual plans only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans, $10,000+ annual commitment)
- Purchase orders (Enterprise plans with approved credit)

### How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Select cancellation reason (helps us improve)
4. Confirm cancellation
5. You retain access until the end of current billing period
6. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation

### What is your refund policy?
Our refund policy:
- **First 30 days**: Full refund, no questions asked
- **After 30 days**: No refunds, but you can cancel anytime
- **Annual plans**: Prorated refund within first 90 days
- **Exceptions**: Refunds may be granted for service outages
- **Process**: Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

### Do you offer discounts?
Yes, we offer discounts for:
- **Annual billing**: Save 20% compared to monthly
- **Non-profits**: 30% discount with proof of 501(c)(3) status
- **Education**: 50% discount for accredited institutions
- **Volume**: 15% discount for 100+ users, 25% for 500+ users
- Contact sales@cloudsync.com to apply for discounts

### Can I change from monthly to annual billing?
Yes! To switch:
1. Go to Settings > Billing
2. Click "Switch to Annual Billing"
3. You'll be charged for the remainder of annual subscription
4. Credit applied for unused monthly period
5. Save 20% compared to monthly billing

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
To reset your password:
1. Go to https://app.cloudsync.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
5. Click link and create new password
6. Password must be at least 12 characters
7. If you don't receive email, check spam folder

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
To enable 2FA:
1. Log in to web dashboard
2. Go to Settings > Security
3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
4. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
5. Enter verification code to confirm
6. Save backup codes in secure location
7. 2FA will be required for all future logins

### Can I have multiple accounts with the same email?
No, each email address can only be associated with one CloudSync account. However, you can:
- Be a member of multiple teams with one account
- Link multiple SSO identities to one account (Enterprise only)
- Use email aliases (yourname+work@gmail.com) for separate accounts

### How do I change my email address?
To change your email:
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Click "Change Email Address"
3. Enter new email address
4. Verify current password
5. Check new email for verification link
6. Click link to confirm change
7. Update email on all devices

### How do I delete my account?
To permanently delete your account:
1. Cancel active subscription first
2. Go to Settings > Account
3. Scroll to "Delete Account" section
4. Click "Delete My Account"
5. Confirm by typing "DELETE" in text box
6. Enter password
7. **Warning**: This action is permanent and cannot be undone
8. All files and data will be deleted within 30 days

### How do I manage team members?
For team management:
1. Go to Settings > Team
2. **Add members**: Click "Invite Team Member", enter email
3. **Remove members**: Click X next to member name
4. **Change roles**: Click dropdown next to member (Admin, Member, Viewer)
5. **Transfer ownership**: Settings > Team > Transfer Ownership
6. Only account owner can delete the account

### What are the different user roles?
CloudSync has three user roles:

**Owner** (1 per account):
- Full control over account
- Manage billing and subscription
- Add/remove team members
- Transfer ownership to another admin
- Delete account

**Admin** (unlimited):
- Add/remove team members
- Manage shared folders and permissions
- View team activity
- Cannot manage billing or delete account

**Member** (unlimited):
- Access shared folders based on permissions
- Create and share personal folders
- Invite external collaborators (if enabled by admin)
- Cannot manage team settings

**Viewer** (Business plan and above):
- Read-only access to assigned folders
- Can download files
- Cannot upload, edit, or delete files
- Cannot share folders

## Technical Questions

### What file types are supported?
CloudSync supports all file types, including:
- Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX
- Images: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, RAW, SVG
- Videos: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM
- Audio: MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG
- Archives: ZIP, RAR, 7Z, TAR, GZ
- Code: All text-based file formats
- Any other file type up to plan's file size limit

### What is the maximum file size?
File size limits by plan:
- **Free**: 250 MB per file
- **Professional**: 2 GB per file
- **Business**: 10 GB per file
- **Enterprise**: 50 GB per file (custom limits available)

For files larger than your limit, consider:
- Compressing files before upload
- Splitting large files into parts
- Upgrading to a higher-tier plan

### How long do you keep deleted files?
Deleted file retention:
- **Free plan**: 30 days in trash
- **Professional**: 60 days in trash
- **Business**: 180 days in trash
- **Enterprise**: Custom retention (up to 7 years for compliance)

After retention period:
- Files are permanently deleted
- Cannot be recovered
- Enterprise customers can request extension

### Can I access files offline?
Yes, offline access is available:
- **Desktop**: All synced files are accessible offline
- **Mobile**: Mark files/folders "Available Offline" in app
- **Web**: No offline access (requires internet)

When offline:
- Changes sync automatically when reconnected
- Conflicts resolved when back online
- View, edit, and create files normally

### What are the API rate limits?
API rate limits by plan:
- **Free**: 100 requests/hour
- **Professional**: 1,000 requests/hour (10,000/day)
- **Business**: 10,000 requests/hour (100,000/day)
- **Enterprise**: Custom limits (no hourly limit)

Rate limit headers:
- X-RateLimit-Limit: Your plan's limit
- X-RateLimit-Remaining: Requests remaining
- X-RateLimit-Reset: Time when limit resets

Exceeding limits returns 429 error with Retry-After header.

### Is my data encrypted?
Yes, CloudSync uses enterprise-grade encryption:
- **In transit**: TLS 1.3 encryption
- **At rest**: AES-256 encryption
- **Zero-knowledge encryption**: Available on Enterprise plan
- **Key management**: AWS KMS (can use your own keys on Enterprise)

Security certifications:
- SOC 2 Type II certified
- ISO 27001 certified
- HIPAA compliant (Business and Enterprise plans)
- GDPR compliant

### Which AWS regions do you use?
CloudSync operates in multiple AWS regions:
- **US East (N. Virginia)** - Primary region
- **US West (Oregon)** - Disaster recovery
- **EU (Ireland)** - European customers
- **AP (Tokyo)** - Asia-Pacific customers

Enterprise customers can:
- Choose specific regions for data residency
- Enable multi-region replication
- Request additional regions

### Do you support Single Sign-On (SSO)?
Yes, SSO is available on Business and Enterprise plans:
- **SAML 2.0**: Okta, Azure AD, OneLogin, Google Workspace
- **OAuth 2.0**: For custom integrations
- **SCIM provisioning**: Automatic user provisioning/deprovisioning

To set up SSO:
1. Go to Settings > Security > SSO
2. Choose your identity provider
3. Follow provider-specific setup guide
4. Test with your account
5. Enable SSO for all team members

### Can I integrate with other tools?
Yes, CloudSync integrates with:
- **Productivity**: Microsoft Office, Google Workspace
- **Communication**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Discord
- **CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot
- **Project Management**: Asana, Jira, Trello
- **Development**: GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket

Custom integrations:
- Use our REST API
- Webhooks for real-time events
- SDKs available for popular languages
